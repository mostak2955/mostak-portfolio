About Me

Hello! I’m a dedicated Ethical Hacker and Cybersecurity Specialist with a mission to protect digital environments and make the internet a safer place. My work revolves around identifying and patching vulnerabilities in web applications, networks, and systems before cybercriminals can exploit them.

I am highly skilled in penetration testing, vulnerability analysis, and secure system design. I’ve worked on various security assessments and have a deep understanding of threats based on real-world attack scenarios.

My toolkit includes hands-on experience with tools like Burp Suite, Metasploit, Nmap, Wireshark, and more. I’m also active in the bug bounty community, contributing to responsible disclosure and improving global cybersecurity.

I believe that hacking should be used for good. Ethical hacking isn’t just a skill—it’s a responsibility.

“Let’s secure the future, one vulnerability at a time.”
